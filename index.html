our speciality

Experience the creamy delight of Malai Chaap at Delhi Malai Chaap's Besant Nagar Branch

Malai Chaap is the signature dish at Delhi Malai Chaap, celebrated for its rich, creamy flavors and tender texture. This vegetarian delicacy is crafted from soya chaap marinated in a blend of curd, cream, and aromatic spices, then grilled to perfection, offering a satisfying alternative to traditional meat dishes.

At the Besant Nagar outlet, conveniently located near the beach, you can enjoy this North Indian favorite as a quick takeaway, perfect for a flavorful snack during your seaside stroll. The Malai Chaap here is known for its authentic taste and affordability, making it a popular choice among locals and visitors alike.
