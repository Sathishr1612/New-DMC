Welcome to Delhi Malai Chaap –
Besant Nagar Branch!

At Delhi Malai Chaap, we bring the rich, creamy flavors of North India straight to the heart of Besant Nagar. Known for our signature Malai Chaap, smoky Tandoori delights, and flavorful chaat varieties, we’re proud to serve vegetarian dishes that even non-vegetarians can’t resist!

Our Besant Nagar branch is more than just a food joint – it's a destination for food lovers who crave the bold spices and satisfying taste of Delhi street food. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite, planning a family outing, or just craving some comfort food, our cozy space and mouthwatering menu are sure to make you feel right at home.

Healthy Fast Food Swaps

A Healthier Alternative
to Fast Food

Are you looking for a delicious and nutritious alternative to traditional fast food? Look no further than Delhi Malai Chaap!

Our chaap is made from high-quality soya, making it an excellent source of plant-based protein. Soya is rich in essential amino acids, fiber, and energy, without excess fat, making it a great option for fitness enthusiasts and health-conscious individuals.

At Delhi Malai Chaap, we're committed to serving you the best-tasting and healthiest chaap options. Our menu features a variety of flavors, Malai, Peri peri , Schezwan , Mexican , Aachrai and many more....

Why choose Delhi Malai Chaap?
  • • High in Protein: Soya chaap is an excellent source of plant-based protein, perfect for vegetarians and vegans.
  • • Low in Fat: Our chaap is cooked with minimal oil, making it a healthier alternative to traditional fast food.
  • • Rich in Fiber: Soya is rich in dietary fiber, promoting digestive health and satiety.
  • • Versatile: Enjoy our chaap as a snack, meal, or even as a protein-packed addition to your favorite recipes.

Make the switch to Delhi Malai Chaap today and indulge in a healthier, tastier alternative to fast food!
